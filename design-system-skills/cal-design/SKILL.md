# Cal.com Design System

A clean, calendar-software-first interface: white canvas, black primary CTAs, custom Cal Sans display type, generous whitespace, and product UI fragments shown inside cards.

## Core tokens (cheat-sheet)
- **Primary**: `#111111` (active `#242424`, disabled `#e5e7eb`)
- **Canvas**: white `#ffffff`; soft `#f8f9fa`; card `#f5f5f5`; dark `#101010` / elevated `#1a1a1a`
- **Ink**: `#111111` / body `#374151` / muted `#6b7280`
- **Hairlines**: `#e5e7eb` / `#f3f4f6`
- **Accent**: brand blue `#3b82f6`; success `#10b981`, warning `#f59e0b`, error `#ef4444`, badge orange `#fb923c`
- **Type**: Cal Sans (custom geometric display) for headlines
- **Radius**: soft-rounded cards (~12px); dark navy footer closes long-scroll pages

## How to apply
1. Read `reference/DESIGN.md` for the full token set, typography, and component specs.
2. Let brand voltage come from the Cal Sans display and in-card product UI, not accent color.
3. Close pages with the dark navy footer.

The complete specification lives in `reference/DESIGN.md`.
