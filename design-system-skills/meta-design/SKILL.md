# Meta Design System

A confident product-merchandising voice spanning hardware commerce (Quest, Ray-Ban Meta) and brand surfaces: stark white canvas, full-bleed photographic product cards, and a saturated cobalt for purchase actions.

## Core tokens (cheat-sheet)
- **Primary (cobalt)**: `#0064e0` (deep `#0457cb`, soft `#0091ff`) — in-product purchase actions
- **Button ink**: `#000000` (black primary CTA); secondary is outlined
- **Canvas**: white `#ffffff`; soft `#f1f4f7`
- **Ink**: deep `#0a1317` / `#1c1e21` / charcoal `#444950` / steel `#5d6c7b`
- **Semantic**: success `#31a24c`, attention `#f2a918`, warning `#f7b928`, critical `#e41e3f`
- **Other brand**: fb-blue `#1876f2`, oculus-purple `#a121ce`
- **Type**: Optimistic VF for wordmark/headline; tight three-tier text hierarchy
- **Radius**: pill-shaped 100px buttons; 24–32px card rounding

## How to apply
1. Read `reference/DESIGN.md` for the full token set, typography, and component specs (homepage, PDP, buy-now configurator, accessories).
2. Lead with full-bleed product photography and dual-CTA heroes (black primary + outlined secondary).
3. Reserve cobalt blue for purchase/commerce actions.

The complete specification lives in `reference/DESIGN.md`.
