# Superhuman Design System

A fast-email productivity brand split between an editorial dark indigo-navy hero and a quiet white content body, reading more like a high-end newsletter than a SaaS app.

## Core tokens (cheat-sheet)
- **Primary (indigo navy)**: `#1b1938` (deep `#0e0c1f`)
- **Canvas**: white `#ffffff` / soft `#fafaf8`
- **Ink**: `#292827` / mute `#73706d` / faint `#9a9794`
- **Hero accents**: violet-soft `#c9b4fa` (atmospheric backdrop)
- **Closing CTA band (teal interlude)**: deep `#0e3030` / mid `#155555`
- **Hairlines**: `#e8e4dd`; dark `#3f3a52`
- **Type**: a single proprietary variable display sans, heavy weight ~460–540 with tight tracking
- **Buttons**: tight rounded rectangles; pricing sober and dense

## How to apply
1. Read `reference/DESIGN.md` for the full token set, typography, and component specs.
2. Lead with the editorial dark indigo hero (violet-sky backdrop + portrait); drop to a calm white body.
3. Break the indigo/white rhythm with the deep-teal closing CTA band.

The complete specification lives in `reference/DESIGN.md`.
