# Intercom Design System

An editorial customer-service marketing canvas: soft cream-white ground, charcoal Saans type, floating white card tiles with thin hairlines, and a single confident Fin Orange reserved for the Fin AI brand.

## Core tokens (cheat-sheet)
- **Primary**: `#111111` (on-primary `#ffffff`)
- **Canvas (cream)**: `#f5f1ec`; surfaces white `#ffffff` / `#ebe7e1`; inverse `#000000` / `#313130`
- **Ink**: `#111111` / muted `#626260` / subtle `#7b7b78` / tertiary `#9c9fa5`
- **Hairlines**: `#d3cec6` / `#ebe7e1`
- **Fin Orange (the only chromatic energy)**: `#ff5600` — Fin AI CTA
- **Report palette (data/illustration)**: blue `#65b5ff`, green `#0bdf50`, pink `#ff2067`, lime `#b3e01c`, cyan `#03b2cb`; brand blue `#0007cb`
- **Type**: Saans (proprietary geometric sans), display weight 500 with measured negative tracking
- **Radius**: minimal, 8–16px

## How to apply
1. Read `reference/DESIGN.md` for the full token set, typography, and component specs.
2. Keep cream ground + floating white tiles; let product screenshots dominate, ornament stays rare.
3. Reserve Fin Orange for the Fin AI CTA; use the report palette only in data/illustration.

The complete specification lives in `reference/DESIGN.md`.
