# Wise Design System

A global money-transfer brand reading like a Scandinavian fintech magazine: unusually heavy near-black display type, a vivid lime-green accent, sage neutrals, and rounded white cards on a pale green canvas.

## Core tokens (cheat-sheet)
- **Primary (lime green)**: `#9fe870` (on-primary `#0e0f0c`, active `#cdffad`, neutral `#c5edab`, pale `#e2f6d5`)
- **Ink**: `#0e0f0c` / deep forest `#163300` / body `#454745` / mute `#868685`
- **Canvas**: white `#ffffff`; soft sage `#e8ebe6`
- **Semantic**: positive `#2ead4b`, warning `#ffd11a`, negative `#d03238`
- **Accents**: orange `#ffc091`, cyan `#38c8ff`
- **Type**: very heavy display sans (weight 900 at 64–126px)

## How to apply
1. Read `reference/DESIGN.md` for the full token set, typography, and component specs.
2. Lean on the heavy weight-900 display type as the dominant brand move.
3. Use lime green as the brand accent with forest-ink contrast; keep neutrals sage-tinted.

The complete specification lives in `reference/DESIGN.md`.
